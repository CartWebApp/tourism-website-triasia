Traveling and Transportation in Philippines

Since Taiwan is it’s own independent national island, the only way to travel there is by airlines. In Taiwan, young tourists should not worry about transportation at all! According to 3 additional sources: Taiwanese Secrets, Nickkembel, and Taiwan the Heart of Asia, the transportation options in Taiwan are: high speed rails, trains, buses, renting a car, scooters or motorcycles, taxis, and bicycles. Additionally, lodging and housing in Taiwan comes easy as well. According to Taiwanese Secrets, “. . . places like Taipei, Kaohsiung and Taichung offer the familiar 5-star names like Sheraton, Hyatt and Shangri-La.” This helps open a lot of options for tourists to stay at for the time they are in Taiwan. On the other hand, Nickkembel offers several links that really offer great deals on housing and transportation. These links are: Klook, Booking, Airbnb, Cookly, and Kiwi.com. Overall, young tourists should not have to worry about finding a place to stay or where to find transportation.
